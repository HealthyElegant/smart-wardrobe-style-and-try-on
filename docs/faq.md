Smart Wardrobe: Style & Try-On – FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q: How does Smart Wardrobe help organize my wardrobe?
A: Smart Wardrobe allows you to digitally catalog your clothing with photos and custom details. You can upload pictures of your clothes and tag them by category, color, season, and more. This creates a virtual closet that mirrors your real one. You’ll be able to scroll through all your items on your phone, which makes it easy to plan outfits. The app even lets you track what you’ve worn or create collections of favorites. In short, it gathers all your wardrobe information in one place so you can easily manage and visualize your clothing inventory. Many users find that this feature reveals new outfit possibilities (by seeing all pieces together) and helps them remember items that might otherwise get lost in the back of the closet.

Q: Can Smart Wardrobe suggest outfits based on the weather?
A: Yes! Smart Wardrobe is weather-aware. The app integrates with a weather service to check your local forecast. When you ask for an outfit (or when you open the app each day), it considers the temperature, weather conditions (like rain or snow), and even factors like humidity or wind. Then it suggests an outfit that not only looks good, but is appropriate for the climate. For example, on a hot sunny day you might get a suggestion for a lightweight outfit with breathable fabrics, while a cold day’s recommendation could include layers, a coat, or boots. This way, you’re comfortable and prepared while still being stylish. You won’t have to manually figure out if it’s coat weather – Smart Wardrobe has you covered with intelligent suggestions tailored to the weather.

Q: Is my data (photos of clothes, personal info) secure and private?
A: Absolutely. We take privacy and security seriously. Your wardrobe data – including photos of your clothes and any style preferences – are stored securely with encryption. This means only you (with your account login) can access them. Smart Wardrobe does not share your personal data with third parties without your permission. Any AI processing (like recognizing your clothing in photos) is handled with privacy in mind. We also use secure authentication for your account. In practical terms, this is as secure as storing your photos in a private cloud storage. Rest assured that your personal style data stays personal.

Q: How does the AI choose outfit recommendations for me?
A: The AI stylist inside Smart Wardrobe works by analyzing several things: the clothes you have, the context you provide, and general fashion principles. When you request an outfit (implicitly or by asking), the AI looks at your wardrobe first – it knows what items you own because you’ve uploaded them. Next, it considers the occasion or guidelines you’ve given (e.g., “casual outfit for a picnic” or it’s a weekday morning for work) as well as practical factors like weather conditions for that day. It then tries to find a combination of your clothes that fits all those parameters. The AI has knowledge of what colors and styles pair well (for example, it knows a formal blazer pairs with dress pants, or that sneakers go with casual outfits). It basically filters and matches your items to create a cohesive look. Over time, the AI also learns from your feedback – if you consistently like or wear certain suggestions, it will prioritize those styles. The result is an outfit suggestion that feels tailored to your needs and preferences, without you having to try dozens of options. It’s like an algorithmic fashion expert that gets smarter about your style the more you use it.

Q: What is the virtual try-on feature?
A: The virtual try-on feature lets you visualize outfits on a person (it could be on a generic avatar or even on a photo of yourself) without actually wearing the clothes. It’s akin to a virtual dressing room. Here’s how it works: you select some items from your wardrobe in the app – say a top, bottom, and shoes – and then choose the try-on option. The app will generate an image where those clothes are arranged on a model figure or your own uploaded photo. This gives you an idea of what the combination looks like when worn together. For instance, you can see how that jacket actually layers over a dress, or whether those two patterns clash when on a body. It’s not a 100% perfect rendering, but it’s quite helpful to see the outfit rather than just imagining it. The benefit is that you can play around with style ideas quickly. Unsure about tucking a shirt in or whether a certain pair of pants suits a top? Use virtual try-on to get a preview. It saves time and effort, and can be done anytime, anywhere. Many users enjoy this feature to decide between multiple outfit options – it’s like having a mannequin of yourself to test clothes on.

Q: How do I add my clothes into the app?
A: Adding your clothes to Smart Wardrobe is easy and even fun. Within the app, you’ll find an option to Add Item or Upload Wardrobe. When you tap that, you can use your phone’s camera to take a photo of a garment (or choose a photo from your gallery if you already have one). For best results, lay the clothing item on a plain background or hang it against a neutral wall, and ensure good lighting. Once you snap the photo, the app will detect the item’s outline. You can then input some details like what type of item it is (or let the AI auto-detect it), the color, and which category it belongs to (e.g. “T-shirt”, “Jacket”, “Shoes”). You can also add any notes (like “comfortable” or “formal”). After saving, that item’s entry is created in your digital wardrobe. Repeat this for all the pieces you want to include – you don’t have to do it all at once; you can start with your favorites or new purchases and build the catalog over time. The AI will automatically categorize and tag items as you add them, so you’ll start seeing sections like Tops, Bottoms, Dresses, etc., populate in the app. It’s rewarding to see your real closet replicated on your device!

Q: Do I have to use voice commands to get outfit ideas?
A: Not at all – voice input is completely optional. Smart Wardrobe is designed to be flexible in how you interact with it. If you’re in a situation where you can’t speak to your device (or just prefer typing), you can use text chat to communicate with the AI stylist. The app has a text box where you can type questions or requests, just like sending a message. The AI will still understand your request and will provide a written response on screen (the same advice it would speak). If you have the voice output turned on, it might also speak the answer, but you can mute your media volume or disable voice if you only want text. Conversely, if you love the hands-free approach, you can use the voice feature – tap the microphone icon and speak your question, and you’ll hear the AI’s answer spoken aloud. It’s up to your comfort and context. Many users switch between the two methods: for example, using voice when at home for convenience, and text when in public. Either way, you get the same helpful fashion advice. Smart Wardrobe aims to accommodate your preferences, whether you’re a texter or a talker.
